To avail a credit card, the documents you need to provide are:
  • Aadhar card/ Voter Id Card (To verify your name, address proof and nationality)
  • Bank Account Details (To transfer the funds)
  • Proof of Your Earnings (To prove that you have a certain earning and capable of repaying the loan.)

If you want a top up on your existing credit card, and have been paying your EMI regularly, then we are always ready to help you with a top up, even though you have an existing one.

As the name suggests itself, a credit card is something you can avail to meet any of your personal needs. Some of the key features of credit card are:

  • Fast Transfer of Funds
  • Minimum Documentation Required
  • Flexible Loan Amount and EMI
  • Flexible Usage

You can repay your credit card at your convenience. The only thing you have to do is to select the method you want to choose, to repay your loan. Some of the methods available to repay your EMI as of now are: Automatic Payment – You can set up auto-pay through Internet banking, and the amount will be debited from your account on the mentioned day. Manual Online Transfer – You can transfer your funds online by yourself to repay your EMI as well. Cheques – You can also use post-dated cheques or drafts to repay your EMI if that’s convenient for you. Standing Instructions – You can instruct your bank to make the payment for your EMI on your behalf on a particular date. Please note that repayment options vary from organization to organization. Hence, it's best to verify the repayment options while availing of your personal loan.

You can easily know your EMI amount from the Credit Card EMI Calculator designed by us. Just enter your loan amount, preferred tenure of the loan, and the interest rate, and the calculator will display your EMI rate.

To be eligible for a credit card you have to meet 3 major criteria. Once you meet them, availing a personal loan is no big deal. The 3 criteria that you must meet to be eligible for a personal loan are:

  • You have to Above 21 Years of Age while applying for the Loan.
  • You must have a minimum of income of Rs. 15000/month.
  • To prove your employment stability you must have worked at least for 2 years in your current profession, be it business or service (including at least 1 year of service in the current organization, if employed.)

Once your credit card is approved and you have signed the loan application, you can choose 2 options to get your funds transferred.
Online Transfer – The amount will be directly sent to your bank account
Payee Cheque – We will send an account payee cheque to your address by post which you can en cash later.
Please note that sending the cheque by post is completely safe and we ensure that they reach to you safely.
