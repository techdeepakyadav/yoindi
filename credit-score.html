As the name suggests itself, a credit card is something you can avail to meet any of your personal needs. Some of the key features of credit card are:

  • Fast Transfer of Funds
  • Minimum Documentation Required
  • Flexible Loan Amount and EMI

You can repay your credit card at your convenience. The only thing you have to do is to select the method you want to choose, to repay your loan. Some of the methods available to repay your EMI as of now are: Automatic Payment – You can set up auto-pay through Internet banking, and the amount will be debited from your account on the mentioned day. Manual Online Transfer – You can transfer your funds online by yourself to repay your EMI as well. Cheques – You can also use post-dated cheques or drafts to repay your EMI if that’s convenient for you. Standing Instructions – You can instruct your bank to make the payment for your EMI on your behalf on a particular date. Please note that repayment options vary from organization to organization. Hence, it's best to verify the repayment options while availing of your personal loan.
